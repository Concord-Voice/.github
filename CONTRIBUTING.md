# Contributing to Concord Voice

Thanks for your interest in contributing to [Concord Voice](https://www.concordvoice.com)! 👋

> [!NOTE]
> Concord Voice is in **alpha**, and most repositories are currently **private**. They open to the public as we reach **beta** (v0.2.0). This guide covers how we work across the organization; a repository's own `CONTRIBUTING.md` overrides it where present.

## Ways to contribute right now

- 💬 Join the conversation on [Discord](https://discord.gg/BZ2fnfsCVw) and [r/ConcordVoice](https://www.reddit.com/r/ConcordVoice/).
- 🐞 File bugs and 💡 share feature ideas through our issue templates (on any repository that's public to you).
- 🔐 Report security issues privately — see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
- ⭐ Back the project on Kickstarter and help us spread the word.

## Working in our repositories

- **Branches** — `feat/…`, `fix/…`, `chore/…`, `docs/…`, `test/…`.
- **Commits** — follow [Conventional Commits](https://www.conventionalcommits.org/) (`feat:`, `fix:`, `docs:`, …) and reference the issue or PR where relevant.
- **Pull requests** — keep them focused, fill out the template, and make sure CI is green before requesting review. Be kind and constructive in review.
- **Licensing** — contributions are accepted under the repository's license: the **Concord Voice Source License (CVSL 1.0)**, which converts to **AGPL-3.0-or-later on 2030-02-15**.

## Code of Conduct

All participation in our spaces is governed by our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Be excellent to each other.

## Questions?

Reach us at [contact-us@concordvoice.com](mailto:contact-us@concordvoice.com) or on [Discord](https://discord.gg/BZ2fnfsCVw).
